Tots Benedict

A variation on a classic

Ingredients

  • 1/2 bag of frozen tater tots
  • 1 pound bacon- diced
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar

    • Hollandaise: Sauce

  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1-2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • hot sauce of your choice

Instructions

  1. Fry the diced bacon until cooked, drain and set aside.
  2. Place the egg yolks and lemon juice in a small double boiler pot. If you prefer less lemony sauce, only use 1 tbsp.
  3. Whisk over the hot water until the egg yolks become glossy.
  4. Whisk in the butter slowly, a little bit at a time, until it's thoroughly incorporated. Keep whisking or the sauce will break.
  5. Once the butter is added, whisk in the hot sauce and cook a minute or two more, whisking the entire time.
  6. To poach your eggs, use a deep frying pan filled with water or a small pot. Add in the vinegar and bring to a boil.
  7. Add some water to your already hot pot to bring it up to about half full. Once the water boils, break the eggs into it.
  8. Cook for 2-3 minutes then remove the eggs with a slotted spoon and drain.
  9. Divide equal amounts of bacon and tater tots between four plates.
  10. Top with a poached egg and Hollandaise.
    11. Serve and enjoy!

Classic Grilled Cheese

A comforting meal, any time.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white American cheese
  • 8 slices country white bread
  • 4 slices yellow American cheese
  • Salted butter, softened

    1. Preheat the griddle to medium heat.
    2. Build the sandwiches with 1 slice of white cheese on 4 pieces of bread and 1 slice of yellow cheese on the other 4 slices of bread.
    3. Close the sandwiches and butter both sides.
    4. Place on the griddle; cover with a metal bowl and let the sandwiches get a nice golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes.
    5. Flip, cover again and cook until the cheese is melted and the sandwiches are golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes.
